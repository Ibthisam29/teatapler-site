Deploy to teatapler.com
Step 1 — Upload to Netlify (2 min)
1. Go to netlify.com → Create a free account
2. From the dashboard, drag both files into the drop zone:
   • TeaTapler_Website.html → rename to index.html first
   • teatapler-admin.html (this file)
3. Your site goes live immediately on a random URL
⚠️ Important: rename TeaTapler_Website.html → index.html before uploading so it loads at the root URL
Step 2 — Connect Your Domain (5 min)
In Netlify:
1. Go to Site Settings → Domain Management → Add custom domain
2. Type teatapler.com → Verify
3. Click "Set up Netlify DNS"
4. Copy the 4 nameserver addresses Netlify gives you (like dns1.p0X.nsone.net)
Step 3 — Update GoDaddy (3 min)
1. Login to godaddy.com → My Products → find teatapler.com
2. Click DNS button → scroll to Nameservers section
3. Click Change → "I'll use my own nameservers"
4. Delete GoDaddy's nameservers
5. Paste Netlify's 4 nameservers → Save

✓ Done — wait 15–60 mins for propagation.
Netlify will auto-enable free HTTPS/SSL.
